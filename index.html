Introduction to Cows

Cows are domesticated animals that are commonly raised for their meat, milk, and other products. They are often found on farms and ranches around the world.

Common Breeds of Cows

  • Holstein
  • Angus
  • Hereford
  • Jersey
  • Simmental

Cow Nutrition

Cows require a balanced diet to stay healthy and produce milk or meat. Their diet typically consists of grass, hay, grains, and supplements.

Fun Facts About Cows

  • A group of cows is called a herd.
  • Cows have a four-chambered stomach.
  • Cows can sleep while standing up.
  • Some cows have a special stomach compartment called a rumen, where they can digest tough plant material.
